The Lucena Position

This is the most important rook endgame position to know. White's goal is to promote the pawn. The key technique is the "bridge" — using the rook to shield the king from checks.

Key idea: Play Rd1+ to drive the enemy king away, then advance your king. Your rook will "build a bridge" by blocking checks from the side.

Try to find the winning move on the board. Move the rook to the correct square.
